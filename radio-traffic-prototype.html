Bexar County Fire Alarm · West
Resolving channels against OpenMHz…
Prototype notes — data pulled live in this browser from api.openmhz.com/bexar (community-run feed, not a BC2FD system — treat as supplementary, never authoritative). Tap a channel name to expand it — all start closed. Tap any call to play it and auto-continue through every newer call in that channel, catch-up style; tap the playing row again to stop. Each lane shows 12 calls at a time — Load more reveals older ones in batches, View full day pages back to local midnight. OpenMHz itself only keeps 30 days regardless of what's shown here. Green dot = channel resolved and polling. Amber = resolved, feed quiet. Red = channel name not found or feed unreachable — this is intentional fail-loud behavior, not a bug: a red lane must never be mistaken for a quiet one.
